tourist places in banyuwangi

KAWAH IJEN

Ijen Crater is an acidic crater lake located at the peak of Mount Ijen with a lake depth of 200 meters and a crater area of ​​5,466 hectares.

PULAU MERAH

Red Island, is a coastal tourist attraction located in Pesanggaran District, Banyuwangi Regency, East Java Province. This beach features a small green hill with red soil near the shoreline.

DE DJAWATAN

De Djawatan is a tourist forest located in Benculuk, Cluring, Banyuwangi Regency. The trees in De Djawatan are rain trees covered in epiphytic plants.

TELUK HIJAU

Green Bay, is a small beach located in the Meru Betiri National Park area of ​​Banyuwangi. The name Green Bay comes from the color of the seawater, which appears greenish, not the blue of most beaches.

PULAU TABUHAN

Tabuhan Island is a small island separated from Java but still part of Banyuwangi Regency. It's not just the island's beauty that makes it quite popular; consistent wind speeds of 20-25 knots in the surrounding area make this Banyuwangi tourist spot renowned as a prime spot for kiteboarding.

ALAS PURWO

Alas Purwo is an ancient site in Indonesia that has now become a national park (TN Alas Purwo). Geographically, Alas Purwo is located at the eastern tip of Java Island, on the southern coast. The Alas Purwo area is located in the Tegaldlimo and Purwoharjo Districts of Banyuwangi Regency, East Java.
