kembiritan village profile

Kembiritan Village is located in Genteng District, Banyuwangi Regency. It covers an area of ~15.16 square kilometers. It is the largest village in Genteng District and still overlaps with the Genteng urban area.

This village boasts several unique cultural traditions, including a celebration of the Prophet Muhammad's birthday. A massive celebration is held during the celebration of the Prophet Muhammad's birthday. This celebration involves riding pedicabs decorated with banana trees and numerous eggs. The eggs are carried around the village, followed by the local community.

banyuwangi regency

Banyuwangi Regency (Hanacaraka: ꦧꦚꦮꦔ, Pegon: باۑوواڠي; Indonesian pronunciation: [baɲuˈwaŋi]) is a regency in the province of East Java, Indonesia. The regency's capital is Banyuwangi District. The regency is located at the easternmost tip of the Indonesian island of Java, specifically in the Tapal Kuda area, bordering Situbondo Regency and Bondowoso Regency to the north, the Bali Strait and Bali Province to the east, the Indian Ocean to the south, and Jember Regency and Bondowoso Regency to the west. Banyuwangi Regency is the largest regency in East Java and the third largest regency on the island of Java.

On the coast of Banyuwangi, Ketapang Port is located, which serves as the main link between Java and Bali. The people who live in this area are the Osing or Wong Blambangan tribe, a Javanese sub-tribe heavily influenced by the Balinese people. The word Osing itself comes from the Balinese word tusing, which means "not." In mid-2023, the population of Banyuwangi was 1,769,234.
