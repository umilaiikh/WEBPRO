banyuwangi local food

SEGO CAWUK

Sego Cawuk is a typical Banyuwangi menu consisting of rice, grated coconut sauce, spicy spices, and various seafood such as eggs, beef jerky or tendon.

SEGO TEMPONG

Sego Tempong is a typical Banyuwangi food consisting of boiled vegetables and side dishes of tofu, tempeh, corn fritters and fried jambal fish and also spicy chili sauce.

AYAM KESRUT

Ayam Kesrut is a typical Banyuwangi soup dish made from chicken with a savory and sour taste.

PECEL RAWON

Pecel Rawon is a combination of pecel and rawon, where the savory and spicy flavors of pecel combine with the delicious rawon sauce.

RUJAK SOTO

Rujak Soto is a unique combination of rujak and soto (meat or tripe). Typically, the rujak is served first, followed by the soto sauce and meat.

TELUR PETIS

Petis Eggs are a food made from eggs, where the eggs are cooked with petis spices.
