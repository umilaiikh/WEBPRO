about me

Hello! Let me introduce myself. I'm Umi Lailatul Khotimah, an Undergraduate of Informatics Engineering student at the Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember with a deep interest in technology and design. I'm currently a member of the Banyubramanta Research Team in the Creative & Branding division, where I contribute to developing technology-based creative solutions. Besides programming, I have a strong passion for photography and nature exploration. My hobby is spontaneously photographing moments, capturing beauty from unique perspectives. My experience as a member of the Nature Lovers organization in high school has shaped my character, which embraces adventure and a deep appreciation for natural beauty. My academic journey has been quite interesting - initially, I was interested in studying forestry at Brawijaya University, but ultimately chose Informatics at ITS. Despite this, my love for nature remains strong to this day. In my free time, I often spend time exploring the beauty of nature while capturing precious moments through my camera lens.
